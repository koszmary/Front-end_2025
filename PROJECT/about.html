About Us

Our Story

Mway Hotels was born out of a simple yet powerful idea: to create a home away from home for travelers seeking comfort, authenticity, and exceptional service. Founded in 2010, our journey began with a single hotel in Oslo, Norway. Thanks to our commitment to quality and guest satisfaction, we quickly gained recognition, allowing us to expand beyond borders.

Today, Mway Hotels proudly operates in Norway, Sweden, and Finland, offering a seamless blend of Scandinavian elegance and warm hospitality. Each of our locations is thoughtfully designed to reflect the unique charm of its surroundings while maintaining the high standards that define our brand. Whether nestled in a bustling city center or overlooking breathtaking Nordic landscapes, every Mway hotel is a gateway to an unforgettable experience.
